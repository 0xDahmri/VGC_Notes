# Regulation H

Regulation H is the current VGC format as of early 2025. It introduced a significant shake-up by allowing **one restricted Legendary** per team, making it the most powerful format in recent years.

## Rules at a Glance

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Format | Double Battles |
| Team Size | 4 of 6 selected at preview |
| Restricted Limit | 1 per team |
| Home Transfers | Allowed |

## Legal Restricted Pokemon

Any Pokemon with a base stat total of 670 or higher from the standard restricted list is allowed. Notable picks:

- **Zacian-Crowned** — still the benchmark. Intrepid Sword + 170 base Attack is oppressive.
- **Calyrex-Ice** — top tier speed control abuser with Glacial Lance.
- **Calyrex-Shadow** — Astral Barrage nukes through Follow Me. Strong in Trick Room.
- **Kyogre** — Drizzle + Water Spout / Origin Pulse. Excellent Water Tera synergy.
- **Groudon** — Drought + Eruption. Mirror of Kyogre, benefits from Ground Tera.
- **Miraidon** — Hadron Engine Electric Terrain enabler. Strong offensive pivot.
- **Koraidon** — Orichalcum Pulse Sun setter. Synergizes with Fire-types naturally.

## Metagame Overview

The format is defined by **weather cores** and **restricted synergy**. Most teams are built around maximizing what their chosen restricted does, then supporting it.

### Dominant Archetypes

**Rain (Kyogre)**
Kyogre + Swift Swim abusers (Kingdra, Barraskewda) under rain is the gold standard rain core. Pelipper provides backup weather.

**Sun (Groudon / Koraidon)**
Groudon's Drought and Koraidon's Orichalcum Pulse both set permanent sun. Pairing with Torkoal gives Drought redundancy. Flutter Mane and Chi-Yu excel in sun.

**Trick Room (Calyrex-Ice / Calyrex-Shadow)**
Calyrex-Ice is the most natural Trick Room abuser given its low Speed. Calyrex-Shadow under TR becomes extremely dangerous if opponents can't break room.

**Offense (Zacian / Miraidon)**
Zacian-Crowned leads are straightforward but still effective. Pair with Follow Me support (Jigglypuff, Amoonguss) or wide guard utility.
